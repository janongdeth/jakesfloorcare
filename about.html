About Jake's Floor Care

Family-owned. Locally operated. Doing floors the right way since 2016.

The Story

Just a Guy Who
Cares About Floors

Jake Lazzarino started Jake's Floor Care in January 2016 with a truck, a carpet machine, and a simple idea: show up on time, do the job right, and charge a fair price. No sales pitch. No corporate runaround. Just honest work.

Ten years later, not much has changed. Jake still answers his own phone. He still shows up to every job himself. And he still treats every customer's home like it's his own. That's the whole secret — there isn't one. He just gives a damn.

What started as carpet cleaning grew into tile and grout work, upholstery cleaning, floor waxing, and eventually a full epoxy and concrete coatings operation. Garages, basements, shop floors, commercial spaces — if it's a floor, Jake's probably worked on one like it.

Jake's Floor Care is a sole proprietorship based out of Wolcottville, Indiana, serving the entire northeast Indiana region. It's a family business in the truest sense — no franchise, no investors, no middlemen.
