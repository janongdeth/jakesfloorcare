Floor Care & Epoxy Services in Decatur

Adams County's reliable choice for professional floor work.

Decatur may be a smaller city, but its residents and businesses take pride in their properties — and that starts with the floors. As the Adams County seat with about 10,000 people, Decatur has a strong agricultural economy, a solid manufacturing base, and neighborhoods full of well-kept homes. Jake's Floor Care has been serving this community with honest, quality floor work for over 10 years.

From farm shops that need a durable concrete coating to family homes that are due for a carpet cleaning, we bring the same professional-grade equipment and attention to detail to every job in Decatur. Jacob Lazzarino runs each project personally, so you always know who's responsible for the work.
