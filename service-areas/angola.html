Floor Care & Epoxy Services in Angola

Quality flooring work for Steuben County's county seat.

Angola sits in the northeast corner of Indiana, surrounded by lakes and the kind of rural beauty that draws people to Steuben County. But lake homes track in sand and grit, and those beautiful cabin floors take a beating. That's where Jake's Floor Care comes in. We've been helping Angola homeowners and businesses maintain their floors for over 10 years — from carpet cleaning in lakeside rentals to epoxy coatings in agricultural shops.

As the Steuben County seat with about 9,000 residents, Angola has a mix of residential neighborhoods, a small but busy downtown, and the Trine University campus. Whatever your flooring situation, owner Jacob Lazzarino and the Jake's Floor Care team bring the right equipment and know-how to get it done properly the first time.
