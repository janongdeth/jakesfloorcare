Floor Care & Epoxy Services in Fort Wayne

Professional flooring solutions for Allen County's largest city.

Fort Wayne is the heart of northeast Indiana, and Jake's Floor Care is proud to be one of the area's most trusted flooring service providers. From downtown offices along Calhoun Street to family homes in Aboite Township, we handle everything from deep carpet extraction to high-performance epoxy garage floors. As the Allen County seat with a population of over 270,000, Fort Wayne deserves flooring professionals who show up on time, do the work right, and stand behind every job.

Owner Jacob Lazzarino has been serving Fort Wayne customers for over a decade. Whether you need tile and grout restored in your restaurant, a metallic epoxy finish for your showroom, or just a thorough carpet cleaning before the holidays — we bring the equipment, the experience, and the BBB A+ rating to back it up.
