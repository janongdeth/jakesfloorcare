Floor Care & Epoxy Services in Auburn

Serving the DeKalb County seat and surrounding communities.

Auburn is known for its automotive heritage and strong sense of community, and Jake's Floor Care is proud to serve this DeKalb County seat. With a population around 13,000, Auburn has a healthy mix of historic downtown commercial buildings and newer residential developments — all of which need reliable floor care at some point. We handle everything from restoring tile in older storefronts to installing high-performance epoxy in modern garages and workshops.

Our team travels regularly to Auburn from our base in Wolcottville, and many of our longest-standing customers are right here in DeKalb County. If your floors need attention — whether it's a deep carpet clean, a concrete coating for your shop, or a full commercial flooring project — Jake's Floor Care has you covered.
