Floor Care & Epoxy Services in Goshen

Professional flooring for Elkhart County's thriving community.

Goshen is one of the fastest-growing communities in northern Indiana, and with growth comes demand for quality floor care. As a city of about 34,000 people in Elkhart County, Goshen is home to RV manufacturers, thriving small businesses, a vibrant downtown, and neighborhoods that range from historic craftsmen to new construction. Jake's Floor Care has been serving Goshen's residential and commercial flooring needs for over a decade.

The manufacturing presence in the Goshen-Elkhart corridor means we handle a lot of industrial and commercial floor work here — epoxy coatings, concrete treatments, and heavy-duty floor systems. But we're equally at home doing carpet cleaning in a Goshen family room or restoring tile in a downtown restaurant. Whatever the job, Jacob Lazzarino and the team show up prepared and get it done right.
