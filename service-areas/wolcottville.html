Floor Care & Epoxy Services in Wolcottville

Our home base in LaGrange County — where it all started.

Wolcottville is where Jake's Floor Care calls home. This is the LaGrange County community where Jacob Lazzarino started the business over 10 years ago, and it's still where we're headquartered today. With a small-town population of about 1,000, Wolcottville is the kind of place where your reputation is everything — and we've built ours on doing quality work for our neighbors.

From here, we serve all of northeast Indiana, but Wolcottville and the surrounding LaGrange County area will always be our first priority. Whether it's a carpet cleaning for your home, an epoxy floor in your barn or shop, or tile restoration in a local business, you're getting the same crew that lives and works in this community. No middlemen, no subcontractors — just Jake's Floor Care, doing what we do best.
