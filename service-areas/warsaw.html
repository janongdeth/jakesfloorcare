Floor Care & Epoxy Services in Warsaw

Kosciusko County's trusted name in professional flooring.

Warsaw is the orthopedic capital of the world, home to some of the biggest names in medical device manufacturing. But behind every clean room and corporate lobby, there's a floor that needs maintaining — and that's where Jake's Floor Care comes in. We serve the entire Kosciusko County seat, from the industrial parks along US 30 to the lakefront homes around Winona Lake and Center Lake.

With a population of about 15,000, Warsaw has a mix of manufacturing facilities, medical offices, restaurants, and residential neighborhoods that all need different kinds of floor work. Jake's Floor Care handles all of it. From carpet cleaning in a family room to epoxy coatings on a factory floor, owner Jacob Lazzarino brings the same level of attention and craftsmanship to every project.
