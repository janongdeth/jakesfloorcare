Epoxy Flooring

Seamless, durable, and good-looking. The floor your garage or shop deserves.

What We Do

Epoxy Floor Coatings for Garages, Basements & Commercial Spaces

Bare concrete stains, cracks, dusts, and looks like an afterthought. Epoxy flooring changes that. It creates a seamless, rock-hard surface that resists oil, chemicals, impacts, and everyday wear.

We install epoxy floor systems for residential garages, basements, workshops, showrooms, retail spaces, and more. The prep work is where most of the job happens — grinding the concrete, filling cracks, getting the surface profile right — and that's where cheap installers cut corners. We don't.

You pick the color and finish. We handle everything from floor prep to final topcoat. Most residential garage floors are done in 2-3 days.

How It Works

Our Process

01

Surface Prep

Diamond grinding opens the concrete pores and creates the profile needed for epoxy to bond permanently. Cracks and divots get filled.

02

Primer Coat

A penetrating epoxy primer soaks into the concrete and creates the foundation for the system. This is what makes it stick.

03

Epoxy Application

The epoxy base coat goes down at the right thickness. Color flakes or solid color, applied evenly across the entire floor.

04

Topcoat & Cure

A UV-stable polyaspartic or polyurethane topcoat seals everything in for long-term durability and chemical resistance.

Why It Matters

Benefits of Epoxy Flooring

Extremely Durable

Handles vehicle traffic, dropped tools, heavy equipment, and daily abuse without chipping or peeling.

Stain Resistant

Oil, gas, brake fluid, paint — it all wipes up. No more permanent stains soaking into bare concrete.

Easy to Clean

A sealed, seamless surface means dust-mopping and occasional wet mopping is all it takes.

Multiple Colors & Finishes

Solid colors, flake blends, quartz, or go metallic. The options are wide and the results look professional.

Increases Property Value

A finished garage floor is a selling point. It makes the space feel intentional, not like an afterthought.

Long-Lasting

Properly installed epoxy floors last 10-20+ years in residential use. This isn't a paint job — it's a system.
