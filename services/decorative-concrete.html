Decorative Concrete

Make concrete look like something it's not. For a lot less money.

What We Do

Custom Staining, Stamping & Overlays

Concrete doesn't have to look like concrete. With the right techniques, it can look like natural stone, brick, tile, slate, or wood — at a fraction of the cost of the real thing.

We offer acid staining for rich, translucent color. Concrete overlays to resurface worn or ugly slabs. Stamp patterns for patios and walkways. And custom color matching to tie everything together with your existing design.

This works on both interior and exterior concrete. Basement floors, living areas, patios, pool decks, driveways, retail floors — if it's concrete, we can make it look like almost anything else. Each project is custom, and we'll work with you on samples before we start.

How It Works

Our Process

01

Consultation

We discuss what you're going for — color, pattern, texture. We bring samples and make recommendations based on the space.

02

Surface Prep

Grinding, cleaning, and repairing the existing concrete so it's ready to accept the decorative treatment.

03

Application

Stain, overlay, or stamp goes down. Color is layered and built up for depth and variation that looks natural, not painted on.

04

Seal & Protect

A clear sealer locks in the color and protects the surface from wear, UV, and moisture.

Why It Matters

Benefits of Decorative Concrete

Fraction of the Cost

Get the look of natural stone, brick, or tile without the material and labor costs of the real thing.

Unlimited Design Options

Colors, patterns, textures — the combinations are endless. Custom-matched to your space and style.

Interior & Exterior

Works on basement floors, living rooms, patios, pool decks, driveways, and commercial spaces.

Durable

When sealed properly, decorative concrete handles traffic, weather, and daily use without losing its look.

Low Maintenance

Sealed decorative concrete is easy to clean and doesn't require the upkeep of natural stone or pavers.

Adds Property Value

A finished, decorative floor or patio is a visual upgrade that buyers and tenants notice.
