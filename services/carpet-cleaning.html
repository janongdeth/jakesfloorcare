Carpet Cleaning

Deep extraction cleaning that actually gets your carpets clean — not just wet.

What We Do

Professional Carpet Cleaning for Homes & Businesses

Vacuuming only gets the surface. Over time, dirt, allergens, pet dander, and bacteria work their way deep into carpet fibers where regular cleaning can't reach. That's where we come in.

Jake's Floor Care uses truck-mounted hot water extraction — the method recommended by every major carpet manufacturer — to pull out what's buried in your carpet. We've been doing this across NE Indiana for over 10 years, and we treat every job like it's our own home.

Whether it's a single room in your house or an entire office building, we'll get your carpets looking and smelling like new. No pushy upsells, no bait-and-switch pricing. Just honest work.

How It Works

Our Process

01

Inspection

We walk your space, identify stains, high-traffic areas, and fiber types so we know exactly what we're working with.

02

Pre-Treatment

Problem spots get a targeted pre-spray that breaks down embedded soil and stubborn stains before the main clean.

03

Deep Clean

Truck-mounted hot water extraction flushes out dirt, allergens, and bacteria from deep in the carpet fibers.

04

Speed Dry

High-velocity air movers cut dry time so you're back on your carpets in hours, not days.

Why It Matters

Benefits of Professional Carpet Cleaning

Removes Allergens

Extracts dust mites, pollen, and pet dander trapped deep in carpet fibers that vacuuming leaves behind.

Extends Carpet Life

Regular deep cleaning prevents fiber breakdown and keeps your carpet looking newer for years longer.

Eliminates Odors

Gets rid of trapped smells from pets, spills, and everyday use — not just masks them with fragrance.

Healthier Indoor Air

Dirty carpets circulate particles every time you walk on them. Clean carpets mean cleaner air in your home.

Stain Removal

Professional-grade solutions and equipment handle stains that consumer products can't touch.

Fast Dry Times

Our truck-mounted system extracts more water, so your carpets dry in 4-6 hours instead of 24+.
