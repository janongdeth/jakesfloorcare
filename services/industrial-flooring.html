Industrial Flooring

Forklifts, chemicals, heavy machinery. Your floor needs to keep up.

What We Do

Heavy-Duty Coatings for Warehouses, Manufacturing & Processing Facilities

Industrial floors deal with things residential and commercial floors never see — forklift traffic, chemical spills, thermal shock, impact from heavy loads, and strict regulatory requirements. A standard epoxy won't cut it.

We install industrial-grade floor coating systems designed for these environments. High-build epoxies, urethane cements, novolac systems for chemical resistance, and MMA (methyl methacrylate) systems for fast-return-to-service applications.

We work with warehouses, distribution centers, manufacturing plants, food processing facilities, breweries, automotive shops, and any facility where the floor is part of the operation. We can also install safety markings, aisle striping, and anti-slip zones as part of the system.

How It Works

Our Process

01

Facility Assessment

We evaluate the concrete condition, chemical exposure, traffic patterns, and regulatory requirements specific to your facility.

02

System Design

We specify the right coating system based on your environment — chemical resistance, thermal shock, impact, and return-to-service timeline.

03

Prep & Install

Shot blasting or diamond grinding to profile the slab. Multi-layer coating system applied per manufacturer specs. Safety markings installed.

04

Inspect & Commission

Adhesion testing, thickness verification, and a final walkthrough. We provide documentation and maintenance guidelines.

Why It Matters

Benefits of Industrial Flooring

Chemical Resistant

Novolac and urethane cement systems resist acids, caustics, solvents, and other harsh chemicals.

USDA Compliant Options

Food-safe, seamless flooring systems that meet USDA and FDA requirements for food processing areas.

Anti-Slip & Safety

Textured finishes, safety striping, and color-coded zones for OSHA compliance and worker safety.

Handles Heavy Loads

Forklift traffic, pallet jacks, heavy machinery — industrial coatings are built for constant heavy use.

Fast Return to Service

MMA and polyaspartic systems can be installed and cured over a weekend, minimizing production downtime.

Easy to Maintain

Seamless surfaces eliminate dirt traps. Power washing and squeegee cleaning is all most facilities need.
