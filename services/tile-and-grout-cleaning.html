Tile & Grout Cleaning

Bring your tile back to life without ripping anything out.

What We Do

Deep Cleaning for Tile & Grout That Actually Lasts

Mopping your tile floor doesn't clean grout. It pushes dirty water into it. Over time, grout lines darken, mold starts to form, and your tile floor looks ten years older than it is.

We use high-pressure steam extraction and commercial-grade cleaning solutions to pull years of buildup out of your grout lines. The difference is visible immediately — no exaggeration.

After cleaning, we can apply a professional sealant that keeps grout looking clean and makes future maintenance a lot easier. Works on bathroom floors, kitchen backsplashes, shower surrounds, entryways — anywhere you've got tile.

Tile & grout cleaning photo
How It Works

Our Process

01

Assessment

We check tile type, grout condition, and identify any problem areas like cracked grout or mold before we start.

02

Apply Solution

A commercial cleaning solution goes down and dwells on the grout, loosening years of embedded dirt and discoloration.

03

Scrub & Extract

High-pressure steam and mechanical scrubbing pull the grime out of grout lines while rinsing the tile surface clean.

04

Seal

We apply a penetrating sealant to protect grout from future staining and make your next cleaning much easier.

Why It Matters

Benefits of Professional Tile & Grout Cleaning

Restores Original Color

Grout that looks brown or black is almost always just dirty. We bring back the original shade.

Prevents Mold & Mildew

Especially in bathrooms and wet areas, clean sealed grout stops mold from taking hold.

Extends Tile Life

Buildup and moisture damage grout over time. Regular cleaning and sealing prevents costly repairs.

Eliminates Bacteria

Kitchen and bathroom floors harbor bacteria in grout. High-temp cleaning kills what mopping can't.

Cheaper Than Replacement

A fraction of the cost of tearing out and re-tiling. Most floors just need a good cleaning, not a renovation.

Immediate Results

You'll see the difference as we work. No waiting for products to "activate" or dry for days.
