Commercial Flooring

Your business runs on these floors. They should be able to handle it.

What We Do

Flooring Solutions Built for High-Traffic Commercial Spaces

Commercial floors take a beating. Hundreds or thousands of feet a day, rolling carts, chairs, spills, cleaning chemicals — and they still need to look presentable. That's a different set of demands than a residential floor.

We work with offices, retail stores, restaurants, medical facilities, churches, schools, and other commercial spaces across NE Indiana. Our services include epoxy coatings, concrete coatings, floor waxing, carpet cleaning, and tile restoration — basically anything your commercial floor needs.

We understand that downtime costs you money. We schedule around your business hours, work efficiently, and get the floor back in service as fast as possible. No dragging jobs out.

How It Works

Our Process

01

Site Assessment

We walk the space, assess the floor condition, discuss your needs, and figure out the right approach for your environment.

02

Custom Plan

We put together a scope of work with clear pricing, timeline, and scheduling that minimizes disruption to your business.

03

Execute

Our crew handles the work — often after hours or on weekends — and keeps the area contained and organized.

04

Final Walkthrough

We walk the finished floor with you, make sure everything meets expectations, and hand off care instructions.
