Upholstery Cleaning

Your couch sees more action than your carpet. Time to give it some attention.

What We Do

Professional Furniture & Upholstery Cleaning

Think about how much time you spend on your couch. Now think about the last time it was actually cleaned. Not spot-treated with spray from the store — actually cleaned. For most people, the answer is never.

Upholstered furniture collects body oils, pet hair, food particles, dust mites, and bacteria just like carpet does. We use hot water extraction and fabric-safe cleaning solutions to pull all of that out, leaving your furniture looking and smelling fresh.

We clean couches, loveseats, recliners, dining chairs, office furniture, and more. We check the fabric type first and adjust our process so nothing gets damaged. Safe for microfiber, cotton, linen, polyester, and most specialty fabrics.

How It Works

Our Process

01

Fabric Check

We identify the fabric type and test cleaning solutions in a hidden spot to make sure there's no discoloration or damage.

02

Pre-Treat & Agitate

Stains and high-contact areas get a targeted pre-spray. We gently agitate to loosen embedded soil before extraction.

03

Extract & Dry

Hot water extraction pulls dirt and moisture out. We leave your furniture damp, not soaked — typically dry within a few hours.

Why It Matters

Benefits of Upholstery Cleaning

Removes Stains

Coffee, wine, food, ink, pet accidents — professional equipment handles stains that consumer sprays can't budge.

Eliminates Odors

Gets rid of pet smell, body odor, and food smells trapped in fabric rather than covering them up.

Reduces Allergens

Dust mites, pollen, and pet dander live in your furniture. Extraction cleaning removes them from the fibers.

Extends Furniture Life

Regular cleaning prevents fiber wear and color fading. Your furniture looks newer for longer.

Safe for All Fabrics

We adjust our approach for each material. Delicate fabrics get gentle treatment, tough fabrics get the full clean.

Healthier Home

Your family sits on this stuff every day. Clean furniture means cleaner air and a healthier living space.
